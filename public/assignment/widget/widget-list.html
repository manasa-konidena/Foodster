The start of Summer is an ideal time to inject some colour into your cooking. These beautiful recipes from some of our favourite online bloggers give a great lesson in making the most of the colours and flavours of your ingredients. Starting off with a classic summer dish. Summer Porridge Recipe

Chop the hazelnuts. Grate the apples into a bowl leaving the skin on. mix with the lemon juice. Add all the other ingredients except for the berries and nuts, then taste. You may wish to add more condensed milk if not sweet enough. Serve it an hour after making to let the oats soak with plenty of fresh berries and the nuts sprinkled on top. Serves 2
